About CS:GO

Burning Defender Olofmeister

There’s a yellow house/crate on Overpass’ Plant B. And it has a CT agent depicted in flames. This is the first ever graffiti CS:GO. It appeared after Olof “olofmeister” Kajbjer defused a C4 while being on fire from a Molotov cocktail in the semi-finals of ESL One Cologne 2014.

The Swedish player disarmed the explosive a split second before dying to snatch the fifteenth round from Dignitas. He made it 15:14. Olof’s Fnatic also took the following round. Winning the game.

This selfless defuse brought the team to victory in the match and, ultimately, in the entire Major. In the finals, Fnatic defeated Ninjas in Pyjamas with a score of 2:1.
