About CS:GO

Fnatic Crazy Quad AWP

During the 33rd round of the grand final of ESL One: Cologne 2015 between Fnatic and Team EnVyUs, Fnatic bought four AWPs and had their entire team, except for JW, set their sights on middle. Although the play only resulted in one kill - pronax taking down apEX - the strategy was so unconventional that viewers and casters alike were amazed.

Valve added a permanent graffiti on a wall near T spawn, depicting a Terrorist holding 4 AWPs simultaneously. However, when Dust II was updated in the October 10, 2017 patch, the graffiti was removed.
